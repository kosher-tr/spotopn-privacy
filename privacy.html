Privacy Policy

Last updated: 22 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the SpotOn app ("SpotOn", "the app", "we", "us") collects, uses, and protects your information. By using the app, you agree to the practices described below.

Age Requirement

SpotOn is intended for users aged 18 and older. The app is not directed at children, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under the age of 18. If we learn that we have collected such information, we will delete it.

Information We Collect

  • Account information: When you register, we collect your email address and basic account details.
  • Camera and video data: The app uses your device's camera to detect your body pose during workouts. Video recorded during workouts is uploaded to and stored on our servers to provide and improve the app's exercise-analysis features.
  • Workout and training data: We collect information about your workouts, exercises, performance metrics, and related activity within the app.
  • Technical data: We may collect basic technical information such as device type and app usage needed to operate and improve the service.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide the app's core features, including camera-based body-pose detection and workout analysis.
  • To create and manage your account.
  • To store and display your workout history and progress.
  • To maintain, improve, and troubleshoot the app.
  • To communicate with you about your account or the service.

Camera and Video

The app requests access to your camera solely to detect your body pose during exercises. Video captured during workouts is transmitted to and stored on our servers. We use this data to power the app's features and to improve the accuracy of our pose-detection technology. We do not sell your videos.

Data Storage and Security

Your data, including recorded workout videos, is stored on our servers. We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information against unauthorized access, loss, or misuse. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data only with service providers that help us operate the app (for example, cloud hosting), and only as necessary to provide the service, or where required by law.

Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data, including your account and stored workout videos. To make a request, contact us at the email below.

Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the service. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
